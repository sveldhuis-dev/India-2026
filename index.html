What to Bring

Packing List

Late Oct–early Nov in North India (Delhi/Agra/Jaipur): warm days (75–85°F), cool mornings/evenings (50–60°F), low humidity. Udaipur similar. Mumbai is warmer and more humid year-round (80–90°F). Modest dress required for temples.

Clothing

  • Lightweight layers — light jacket/sweater for cool mornings (Delhi/Jaipur/Udaipur)
  • Modest clothing for temples — shoulders/knees covered
  • Comfortable cotton/linen shirts for Mumbai heat
  • 1–2 nicer outfits for special dinners (Jaipur musicians dinner)
  • Scarf or shawl (useful for temple visits, AC, dust)

Footwear

  • Comfortable walking shoes — forts have significant walking/stairs
  • Slip-on shoes — many temples require shoe removal
  • Sandals for hotel/evening wear

Health & Sun

  • Sunscreen SPF 50+
  • Sunglasses, sun hat
  • Hand sanitizer, wet wipes
  • Prescription medications + copies of prescriptions
  • Consult travel doctor — possible vaccinations/malaria prophylaxis
  • Stomach medication (Imodium, Pepto) as precaution

Photography

  • Camera + extra batteries
  • Wide angle lens — Taj Mahal, forts, palaces
  • Extra memory cards
  • Portable charger

Practical

  • Universal power adapter (India: Type C/D/M, 230V)
  • Cash — small USD bills + plan to exchange for INR
  • Copies of passport + eVisa printed
  • Travel insurance documents (Travelex)
  • Earplugs (city noise, early flights)

Documents

  • Passports — valid 6+ months from travel date
  • eVisa confirmation (apply within 30 days of travel)
  • Flight confirmation GV5C9D
  • JourneyQuest itinerary + emergency contact
  • Travelex insurance policy details
  • Hotel confirmations (Oberoi ×3, Taj Mahal Palace)
Insider Knowledge

India Trip Tips

🛂
eVisa TimingApply within 30 days of travel per your notes — not earlier. Mark a calendar reminder for late September 2026 to submit.
🐘
Amber Fort — Jeep vs. ElephantJourneyQuest defaults to jeep transport up to Amber Fort due to animal welfare concerns around elephant rides, though they can arrange it if specifically requested.
🕌
Taj Mahal is Closed FridaysConfirm your Agra day-trip date isn't a Friday — the tour document explicitly notes this closure.
🛕
Temple EtiquetteShoes off before entering most temples. Shoulders and knees covered. A light scarf is useful for impromptu head coverings at certain sites.
💧
Water & Food SafetyPremium bottled water is included throughout the tour. Stick to bottled/filtered water even for teeth-brushing. Hotel restaurants (Oberoi, Taj) are very safe; street food is tempting but higher risk.
💰
Tipping CultureIndia has an active tipping culture for guides, drivers, and hotel staff. Budget roughly $20–30/day per couple for the tour guide and driver combined; small additional tips for hotel staff.
🛍️
Shopping NegotiationBazaar shopping (Chandni Chowk, Jaipur markets) involves negotiation as standard practice. Fixed-price shops (government emporiums) don't negotiate but guarantee authenticity for items like carpets and jewelry.
📅
Holiday Schedule FlexibilityPer the tour document: "some holidays the government announces" may shift the itinerary automatically. Stay flexible on exact day-to-day sequencing — JourneyQuest will adjust as needed.
✈️
The Long HaulBoth international legs are ~26 hours door to door with 2 stops. Factor in significant jet lag recovery — the itinerary wisely builds in a rest period on Day 1 in Delhi.
